Elon Muski

Elon Musk is a South African-born entrepreneur, engineer, and inventor best known for founding SpaceX and co-founding Tesla, Inc., Neuralink, and OpenAI. Born in 1971 in Pretoria, South Africa, Musk moved to the U.S. to pursue studies in physics and economics. He first gained recognition as a co-founder of PayPal, which was later acquired by eBay. At SpaceX, Musk has led the development of reusable rockets and aims to colonize Mars. At Tesla, he has revolutionized the electric vehicle industry. Known for his bold vision and high-risk ventures, Musk is one of the most influential and controversial tech leaders of the 21st century.
